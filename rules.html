How to Play & Rules

Objective

The goal is to get your white shape into the black goal hole in the center of the board.

Controls

  • On Desktop: Click and hold your white shape, drag the mouse away from it to aim, and release the mouse button to shoot.
  • On Mobile: Tap and hold your shape, drag your finger to aim, and lift your finger to shoot.
  • The further you drag, the more powerful your shot will be!

Rules & Scoring

  • You start with 5 Pucks (lives).
  • Scoring for pocketing the puck in the correct hole depends on the number of shots taken in that level:
    • 1st Try: 5000 points
    • 2nd Try: 4000 points
    • 3rd Try: 3000 points
    • 4th Try: 2000 points
    • 5th Try: 1000 points
  • You get a Bonus Puck every 5 levels you complete.

Obstacles & Penalties

  • Obstacle Holes: Avoid the dark brown holes! If your puck falls into one, you lose 1 Puck.
  • Moving Pucks: Beware of the colored pucks moving around the board.
    • Each time you hit one, you lose 500 points.
    • For every 10 hits, you will also lose 1 Puck.
